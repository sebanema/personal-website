About my work

I’m a financial professional with deep experience solving commercial and operational problems for fintech and high-growth startups. I specialise in helping early-stage companies graduate from “scrappy” to “structured,” building financial systems that support real scale.

I now run an independent consulting practice supporting founders with:

  • Establishing clean, reliable financial operations
  • Turning raw data into clear, accurate reporting
  • Setting up investor-ready data rooms for capital raises

Previously, I supported the CFO at Yellow Africa, a company serving over 800,000 families across the continent. Our team oversaw multi-entity finance operations, managed treasury and FX, and built capital-allocation models that shaped funding and investment strategy across the organisation.

What I help founders with

My goal is to bring calm and clarity back into your numbers so you can focus on building.

  • Clean, usable finance dashboards instead of chaotic spreadsheets
  • Financial models investors can actually read
  • Clear insight into cash, risk, and runway
  • Systems that scale so you don’t drown when the business grows

If your financial operations feel messy, reactive, or unclear, you don’t have to work that way. Book a call and I’ll help you get your house in order.
